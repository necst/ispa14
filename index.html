Welcome to ISPA-14!

The 12th IEEE International Symposium on Parallel and Distributed Processing with Applications (ISPA-14) follows the tradition of previous successful ISPA conferences, ISPA-03 (Aizu, Japan), ISPA-04 (Hong Kong), ISPA-05 (Nanjing, China), ISPA-06 (Sorrento, Italy), ISPA-07 (Niagara Falls, Canada) and ISPA-08 (Sydney, Australia), ISPA-09 (Chengdu, China), ISPA-10 (Taipei, Taiwan), ISPA-11 (Busan, Korea), ISPA-12 (Madrid, Spain) and ISPA-13 (Melbourne, Australia).

The objective of ISPA-14 is to provide a forum for scientists and engineers in academia and industry to exchange and discuss their experiences, new ideas, research results, and applications about all aspects of parallel and distributed computing and networking. It will feature session presentations, workshops, tutorials and keynote speeches.

EUC 2014 will take place in Milan, Italy.
